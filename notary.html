Primary service

Remote Online Notary

Complete your notarization securely online from wherever you are. This is the fastest and most scalable option for clients who want convenience, speed, and a structured process.

  • Available to Tennessee and Georgia clients, subject to document suitability
  • Ideal for time-sensitive appointments
  • Secure identity verification and scheduled online session
  • Clear appointment flow and direct communication

Secondary service

Mobile Notary — Tennessee Only

In-person notarization is available within Tennessee by appointment. Mobile service is best for clients who need location-based convenience at a home, office, or agreed meeting point.

  • Tennessee only for in-person appointments
  • Travel-based pricing applies
  • Scheduled service with minimum visit fee
  • Best for local, document-ready appointments
