Premium add-on

Wet Notarization + Overnight Return

For clients who require a physical notarized copy following a digital workflow, Ralwyn may provide wet notarization of a printed document where permitted by applicable law and document requirements.

  • Available only when document type and workflow are appropriate
  • Physical handling and mailing service billed separately
  • Overnight return available for time-sensitive requests
  • Final availability confirmed during intake

Client responsibility

Document acceptance and compliance

Clients are responsible for confirming that their documents are eligible for remote notarization and, if requested, that a printed and wet notarized copy will be accepted by the receiving party.

Ralwyn provides notarization services in compliance with Tennessee law. Acceptance of notarized documents by third parties, institutions, agencies, or jurisdictions is outside the control of Ralwyn.
