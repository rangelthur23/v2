Pricing

Transparent Tennessee pricing with the notarial act separated clearly.

In-Person Notary (Active)

  • Notarial Act (Per Stamp): $25 — Tennessee state maximum
  • Travel Fee: none by default for fixed-location in-person appointments

The $25 fee applies only to the notarial act. Final timing and location must be confirmed before the appointment is scheduled.

Mobile Notary (Active)

  • Notarial Act (Per Stamp): $25 — Tennessee state maximum
  • Travel Fee: $25 base, including the first mile
  • Additional Mileage: $1 per mile after the first mile

The $25 fee applies only to the notarial act. Travel charges are separate and reflect time, distance, scheduling, and service delivery. Final travel charges are confirmed before the appointment is scheduled.

Remote Online Notary (Coming Soon)

Remote Online Notarization is not yet active. Tennessee approval is in progress.

Important: when remote service launches, the notarial act will remain capped at $25 per stamp under Tennessee law. Any additional administrative, technical, identity-verification, or operational charges will be presented separately and clearly before booking.

How it works

In-person workflow

  • 1. Submit your request
  • 2. We review availability and confirm the meeting location
  • 3. You receive appointment confirmation
  • 4. We complete notarization at the confirmed appointment

Mobile workflow

  • 1. Submit your request with the signing address
  • 2. We review availability and confirm travel pricing
  • 3. You receive appointment confirmation
  • 4. We travel to the confirmed location and complete notarization

Remote online notary launch workflow

  • 1. Join the waitlist now
  • 2. Receive launch notice after Tennessee approval
  • 3. Review live pricing and compliance details
  • 4. Book a secure video notarization once active
