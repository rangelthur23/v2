About Ralwyn

Experience, structure, and practical guidance.

Ryan Headshot

Founder

Ryan

Ryan is a graduate of the Coles College of Business at Kennesaw State University. Coles College of Business is among the top 2% of business schools worldwide with AACSB accreditation in both business and accounting. He earned his BBA in Management and brings more than 15 years of operational accounting experience across multiple companies in the commercial real estate and construction industries. During that time, he developed strengths in leadership, operations, project coordination, problem-solving, and organizational strategy.

Lisa Headshot

Founder

Lisa, RN-C OB

Lisa is a registered nurse licensed in the state of Tennessee with 15 years of experience in Labor and Delivery, postpartum, and women’s services. She earned her Associate of Science in Nursing in May 2011 and her Bachelor of Science in Nursing in July 2015. She also holds the RN-C OB specialty credential, reflecting specialized knowledge in inpatient obstetric nursing. For the past five years, she has served as a Clinical Staff Leader, further developing her leadership and management experience. She also holds BLS, ACLS, and NRP certifications.
