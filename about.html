About Ralwyn

Experience, structure, and practical guidance.

Ralwyn is built around clarity, structure, and measurable progress, with a people-focused approach grounded in real-world experience.

Founder

Ryan

Ryan brings more than 15 years of experience across operational accounting, treasury, business operations, and financial administration in construction, commercial real estate, and small business environments.

He is a graduate of the Coles College of Business at Kennesaw State University, where he earned a BBA in Management. His background includes acquisition support, AP and AR oversight, multi-entity payment operations, financial program administration, and process-driven coordination across complex workflows.

He is known for disciplined execution, practical problem-solving, and bringing clarity to financial and operational challenges that need structure, follow-through, and sound judgment.

Founder

Lisa, RN-C OB

Lisa is a registered nurse licensed in Tennessee with 15 years of experience in Labor and Delivery, postpartum, and women’s services. She also holds the RN-C OB specialty credential, reflecting advanced knowledge in obstetric nursing.

For the past five years, she has served as a Clinical Staff Leader, where her work has included staff coordination, scheduling, communication between nursing staff and physicians, conflict resolution, and daily clinical oversight.

Her leadership style is grounded, practical, and patient-centered. She brings steady judgment, strong communication, and real-world management experience shaped by fast-moving clinical environments.

Approach

Grounded, clear, and built to be useful.

Ryan and Lisa bring together experience in business operations, accounting, leadership, and healthcare to support clients with practical, experience-based guidance. Together, they offer a practical, people-focused perspective grounded in real-world experience.
